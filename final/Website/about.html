About

The Cinema Rolls Movie Podcast was created in October 2014 as a platform for movie fans, Matt Liebetrau and Alex Newman, to discuss the latest movie news, rumors, and reviews. Desperate to start a movie podcast but lacking someone with the knowledge and love for movies to host it with, Matt took to the internet to find a co-host. Posting on the /r/movies subreddit got Alex's attention and after an initial Skype meeting and several pun-filled iterations of potential names, Cinema Rolls was born.

New episodes of the podcast are posted on Mondays and are typically around an hour in length. To listen to all our episodes, check out our Archive page. If you'd like to get in touch, feel free to drop us a line on our Contact page.
