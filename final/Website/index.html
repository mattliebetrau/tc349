About Cinema Rolls

The Cinema Rolls Movie Podcast is a weekly, (roughly) hour-long podcast that celebrates the joy of being a movie fan. Hosts Matt and Alex get together one night a week to talk about what they've recently watched, the movie news from that week, and review a newly released or topical movie.
