Ep. 006: Planes, Trains and Automobiles Review, Jurassic World teaser, Peanuts trailer, and More

On this episode of the Cinema Rolls Movie Podcast, Matt and Alex are once again joined by special guest, Steve Jencks, and give their reviews of Planes, Trains and Automobiles and discuss the Jurassic World teaser, Sony dropping the Steve Jobs movie, the Peanuts trailer, and Angelina Jolie retiring from acting. Just in time for Thanksgiving, they also discuss what movie-related things they're thankful for.
